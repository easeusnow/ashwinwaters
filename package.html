Packaged Drinking Water

Packaged Drinking Water is best known for its remarkable consistency in quality over the years, Now the painstaking efforts are brought to you in your glass of water. Features of the water are :

  • Complete bottled water service that is safe and hygiene
  • Conforms to Stringent guidelines of BIS
  • Goes through 8 stages of purification process
  • Carefully bottled in a food grade jar with no off taste or off odour
  • Comfort of 20 Ltr jar delivered at your doorstep at no extra cost
  • Dispensers are available in 2 sizes for domestic and commercial use
  • Water is available in 20 Ltr Jar and also available in convenient pack size of 2 Ltr, 1 Ltr, 500 ml and 250 ml
  • Every container is coded with Batch No and Date or Manufacturing and Expiry with inkjet coding machine

We manufacture Hot and Cold Dispenser which are :

  • High impact, durable, fast flow and ABC plastic faucets
  • Quiet air cooled static type condenser
  • Smart looking and light weight
  • Lower power consumption

We also manufacture Domestic Dispenser which are :

  • Solid, transparent PET container
  • Easy and convenient to use
  • Made from food grade non toxic material
  • Lower power consumption
