Quality and Standards

Packaged Drinking Water goes through rigorous 8 stage purification process to meet the most stringent Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) for water purity vide IS 14543. Our water treatment plant is equipped with state of the art online dosing, multistage micron filtration, RO processing and UV sterilization.

Distribution Network

We have franchisee all over Bihar for our packaged drinking water products. We are spreading our distribution network more to enable the water and beverage available within your reach.

Water Facts

When it comes to your health, the quality of your drinking water is just as important as that of the quantity. Hence our endeavor is to manufacture packaged water to the international standard of water for human consumption.
